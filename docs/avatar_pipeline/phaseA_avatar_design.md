## Shared Artistic Notes
- **Palette**: Keep saturated magenta/pink/purple gradient consistent with storyboard.
- **Materials**: Sequins via normal map/glitter shader, faux fur using hair cards or fluffy alpha texture.
- **Lighting**: Warm golden rim + purple fill to match reference renders.
- **Rig Constraints**: Maintain R15 vertex groups; clothing meshes will be separate MeshParts parented to corresponding bones.
